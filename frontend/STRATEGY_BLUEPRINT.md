## ðŸ—ï¸ Phase 1: Website Architecture

The site structure is designed to maximize **Pageviews Per Session** (good for ads) and **Crawlability** (good for SEO).

### 1. Homepage Layout (The "Magazine" Look)
*   **Hero Section (Above Fold):** A "Bento Grid" layout featuring the top 1 Breaking News story + 4 Trending Stories.
    *   *Why:* Keeps the site fresh and reduces bounce rate.
*   **Sticky Sidebar (Right Side):**
    *   Widget 1: "Trending Now" (Most popular posts).
    *   Widget 2: **300x600 Skyscraper Ad** (Highest earning ad slot).
    *   Widget 3: Newsletter Signup.
*   **Feed Section:** Infinite scroll of "Latest News" mixed with **In-Feed Native Ads** every 3 posts.

### 2. Navigation Structure
*   **Home**
*   **AI Tools** (High CPC: Targeting "Best AI for business/coding")
*   **Tech News** (High Volume: "Jio", "5G", "WhatsApp updates")
*   **Tutorials** (Evergreen: "How to...", "Fix errors")
*   **Reviews** (Affiliate intent: "Best mobile under 20k")
*   **Deals** (Seasonal: "Big Billion Days", "Amazon Sale")

### 3. Single Article Structure (The Money Maker)
*   **Header:** H1 Title + Author + Last Updated Date.
*   **Ad Slot 1:** Leaderboard (728x90) immediately under the title.
*   **Intro:** Hook (Problem) -> Solution -> "Read more".
*   **Table of Contents:** Auto-generated (Jump links improve SEO).
*   **Content Body:** Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences).
*   **Ad Slot 2:** In-Article (300x250) after paragraph 4.
*   **FAQ Section:** JSON-LD Schema optimized questions.
*   **Ad Slot 3:** Matched Content / Native Ad at the bottom.
*   **Sticky Footer:** "Share this post" + Next Article button.
